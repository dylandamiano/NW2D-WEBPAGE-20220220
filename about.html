Background Information


Naval Warfare 2D was a project started back in December of 2021 as part of a Tech Prep Showcase.

The game was developed by Dylan Damiano and his team.



Diving In...


As of right now, Naval Warfare 2D consists of the following:

  • 1 Map
  • 2 Character Classes
  • A baked potato processor


The game was developed by Dylan Damiano and his team.
